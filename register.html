DriveShield

Registration

To register for DriveShield, please visit your nearest Regional Transport Office (RTO) with the following important documents:

  • Valid Driver's License
  • Vehicle Registration Certificate (RC)
  • Proof of Insurance
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Address Proof (Aadhaar Card, Passport, or Utility Bill)
  • PAN Card

The RTO officials will guide you through the DriveShield registration process and help you set up your account.
